Gentry Stein

Who is Gentry Stein?

Gentry Stein is a well-known four-time national yoyo champion and two-time world champion. He retired from competing since covid season, but he still participates in yoyo contests as a demo, judge, sponsor, or through the people from the yoyo team he formed, Yoyochampion, and runs his own website where he sells his yoyos.

List of achievements

He has won the 2013 and 2016 national yoyo contests and the 2014 world yoyo contest with the Shutter, his most influential signature yoyo, the 2015 national yoyo contest with the Replay Pro, a cheap durable plastic yoyo, and the 2019 national and world yoyo contests with the Shutter Wide Angle, a wider version of the original Shutter that still keeps the same feel.
