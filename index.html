Who is Xavier Ng?

Xavier Ng is a well-known four-time Singaporean national yoyo champion and currently ranks 5th in the world. He is part of Offset Yoyo, a popular yoyo team consisting of Brandon Vu, Xavier Ng, and Allon Chen.

List of achievements

He has won the 2019, 2023, 2024, and 2025 Singaporean national yoyo contests, the semifinal round for the 2025 world yoyo contest, the 5th, 6th, and 7th Stronger Yoyo Cup episodes, and has finished 5th in the world for 2025 - not that he doesn't have other wins.

What he does

He is on the Offset Yoyo team along with Brandon Vu and Allon Chen. They sell yoyos, make videos, and compete in yoyo contests. Xavier's signature yoyos are the Xenon and Xenon Prime.
